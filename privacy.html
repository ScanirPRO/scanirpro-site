Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 25-09-2025

ScanirPRO does not collect, store, or share your scanned documents. All scans remain securely on your device unless you explicitly share them.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used to capture documents and QR codes.
  • Storage: Used to save, read, and manage scanned files.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to share a file (via WhatsApp, Gmail, Google Drive, etc.), it will be processed by that third-party app. ScanirPRO itself never uploads your files.
